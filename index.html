/83

I currently live in Austin, TX, but Michigan will forever be my home. Eighty-Three Counties of Michigan is a passion project I started a while back that I wanted to give a face lift to. Every county gets an emblem and a short history lesson.

/wings1415

The Detroit Red Wings are a passion for me, so when I went about creating a schedule for the 2014-2015 season, I wanted to make it something special. Looking at NHL.com and trying to wade through the garbage to get to a schedule has always been a huge bummer to me. So I wanted mine to be accessible from all devices and simple to navigate. This was my first foray into fluid grids and a fly out nav.
